1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites function, improve performance, and provide analytics and personalisation features.

2. How we use cookies

  • Essential — required for core site functionality and security.
  • Analytics — help us understand usage and improve the site (consent-based).
  • Advertising — where implemented, to measure and deliver relevant ads (consent-based).

4. Managing your preferences

You can manage non-essential cookies via our banner (when shown) or through your browser settings. To withdraw consent, clear cookies for this site and refresh. For browser-level controls, consult your browser’s help pages.
