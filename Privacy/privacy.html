Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how KSRM Digital (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects and uses information when you visit our website, use our free tools, or contact us. We comply with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who we are

KSRM Digital is a brand initiative of 9th Dimension Digital Ltd (UK). For privacy queries, email: contact@ksrm.digital.

2. Data we collect

  • Usage data: pages visited, referring site, approximate location, device/browser data.
  • Tool inputs: values you enter into our calculators/tools (processed in-browser; we do not store results unless stated).
  • Contact data: name, email, and message if you contact us.
  • Cookies: see our Cookies Policy for details and controls.

3. How we use your data (lawful bases)

  • Provide and improve the site & tools (legitimate interests).
  • Respond to enquiries (consent or legitimate interests).
  • Security, debugging, and prevention of abuse (legitimate interests).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

4. Cookies & tracking

We use essential cookies to run the site. With your consent, we may use analytics and advertising cookies. See the Cookies Policy for categories, purposes, and how to change your preferences.

5. Advertising & analytics

We may integrate third-party services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense via Google Tag Manager. These providers may set cookies and collect usage data subject to their own policies. Where required, we request your consent for non-essential cookies.

6. Sharing your data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with trusted processors (e.g., hosting, analytics, error monitoring, email service) under data processing agreements and only for the purposes outlined here.

7. Storage & retention

Data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or to meet legal obligations. Tool calculations are typically processed client-side and not stored by us unless explicitly stated for a feature.

8. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact contact@ksrm.digital. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO).

9. International transfers

If data is transferred outside the UK, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., UK IDTA or EU SCCs as adopted in the UK) to protect it.

10. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Children

Our site and tools are designed for general audiences and are not directed to children under 13.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, provide notice.

13. Contact us

Email: contact@ksrm.digital
